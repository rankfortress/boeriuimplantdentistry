Sorin Boeriu DDS

A smile captures the essence of who you are. It depicts your personality, happiness, and self worth. So our goal is to create the most exquisite smiles with an important emphasis on providing optimum oral health. We take pride in our patients by investing in the finest technologies to maximize your quality of care.Our practice is highly personalized, with doctors and staff taking time to listen to your needs and getting to know you in the process. We offer complete dentistry services including, but not limited to, check-ups, cleanings, composite fillings, crowns/bridges, root canals, implants, extractions, replacement dentures and cosmetic dentistry. By performing multiple dental procedures in house, in our dental group, we cut down on time a patient waits for treatment and also on the cost of treatment. Our professional team strives to provide you with quality, personalized dentistry and friendly service, and we also look to make the whole process easy. It is very convenient to schedule an appointment, as we offer early morning as well as late evening hours in order to work around your daily routine.
